Welcome to Once Upon a Time in Coventry

The only festival you want to visit in the heart of Coventry.











Join Us

Once Upon a Time in Coventry is a festival celebrating the grand reveal of River Sherbourne. Unknown to many, River Sherbourne has always been quietly running through Coventry however a river with such pride and history behind it should be known to the public. Palmer Lane is one of Coventry’s oldest streets and it deserves to be recognised. Come visit us today!
